    HOW TO STAY AWAY FROM DRUGS?

    Learn Why People Use Drugs?

    People use drugs and alcohol for a variety of different reasons like for relaxation, enjoyment, avoid physical pain, exciement, selfmedicating for mental health condition, due to peer pressure, to experience the positive feelings associated with substances.

    Learn How Addiction Develops

    In general, people can develop tolerance to drugs when they use them repeatedly. An addiction can take over a person`s life, making use of the substance the number one priority over all other factors.
